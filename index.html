Representation of American Citizens in the US Congress (Lower house) and Senate (Upper house)

This project was born out of a premise; that the typical American is under represented in the US government. Particularly in the House or representatives and the US Senate. To look into this, We've created an interactive plot to compare representation - defined here as the number of citizens are represented by 1 member of the body in question - in the US with representation in other countries. We compare with a variety of other countries (other Western (OECD) countries, countries with large populations, countries with small populations) to get a sense of how the US compares with other nations.

Happy viewing

Caveats: Countries that do not have an upper house are not displayed if the upper house is selected in the dropdown menu. The UK which technially does have a upper house (The house of Lords) is excluded here because a lot those seats are filed by appointment and not elected by the citizens and so those memebers cannot be seen to 'represent' the citizens.
